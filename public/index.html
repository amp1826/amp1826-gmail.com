Dear Clients and Friends of Hava Volman Catering,
We wish you and your families strength and good health in the face of the many uncertainties brought on by the COVID-19 crisis.
With Passover traditionally celebrated with family and friends many are anxious about the prospects of celebrating in a different way this year. With the curtailing of traveling and social interactions, we wanted to let you know that we have decided to offer Passover Seder in a Box in NYC (see attached menu which is subject to availability of ingredients). Meals will be prepared in an A-rated commercial kitchen under strict Department of Health Guidelines.
Some things to consider
  • Aside from one to two prep chefs and Hava herself, no one else is involved in the preparation and packaging of meals. The health department guidelines require already cooked foods to be handled with gloves, but we are now using gloves at every step.
  • The coronavirus is very sensitive to heat; meals are cooked at appropriately high temperatures, packed and then refrigerated in packages that are microwave and oven safe. The meal is then reheated by you.
  • One person will deliver meals. You can request no-contact delivery, which simply means the delivery person will leave your food in a safe place of your choosing and alert you when its ready for pickup.
